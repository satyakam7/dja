STEPS TO REGISTER FOR INSTITUTE SWIMMING POOL

For students, staff and faculty

  • The registration form has to be filled by the applicant and signed by the Medical Officer.
  • Upload a scanned copy of the above form using your institute email here.
  • Your form would be verified and we will notify you via an email once it is done.
  • If your form has been approved, you can book the slots on the portal.
  • For booking a slot you have to provide your details, upload your photo and pay the fee online.
  • If your form is disapproved, you have to reupload a correct one in order to book slots.

For relatives of staff and faculty

  • The relative of a non-student member has to be registered here using the email the non-student member with a Govt. Identity proof.
  • The relative would be approved and a relative code would be conveyed via an email on approval.
  • If the relative is disapproved, the relative would have to registered again along with a valid proof of relation.
  • Approved relatives have to fill the registration form get it and signed by the Medical Officer.
  • A scanned copy of the above filled form has to be submitted here using the received relative code and the email of non-student member.
  • The above form would be verified and we will notify you via an email once it is done.
  • Upon approval of the form, you can book the slots on the portal using the received relative code and the email of non-student member.
  • For booking a slot you have to provide your details, upload your photo and pay the fee online.
  • If your form is disapproved, you have to reupload a correct one in order to book slots.
