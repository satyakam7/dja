If you ever notice the Athletics ground in the late sweaty evenings, you’ll see youngsters repeating drills, panting and falling and yet, getting up. You’ll see their legs darting, temples throbbing and if you look close enough you’ll see their invincible spirit. To beat themselves and to win. Blood, sweat and tears that's what it takes to win golds and that's what the Athletics team has given.

From stupendous performance in BOSM'16 to Spardha'16,Athletics at IIT Delhi has grown up humongously.They have truly set an example of sportsmanship and team spirit with smashing performance in prestigious Inter IIT'16.

From two bronzes at Inter IIT'14 to clean sweep in Inter IIT'16,they have come a long way. They have stellar athletes in Dileep pudi who has broken records after records and Rahul jhajhria who has impressed with his hardwork and dedication.

The person behind such unparallel victory with 5 new Inter IIT records,is their coach Neeraj Chaudhary who has given his cent percent to the team.

We have a lot of events to try your hands on such as Short and long distance runs, jumps from long to triple, pole vault, and throws not just shotput and discuss but hammer and javelin too. All at athletics ground only. We Athletics,IIT Delhi invite you all to be part of this legacy.
