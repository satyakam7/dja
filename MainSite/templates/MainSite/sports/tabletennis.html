The institute table tennis team has emerged as a very dominant force to reckon with in the past couple of years. There are two separate table tennis halls for men and women, both located in SAC apart from tables in every hostel. The team practices every day from 6pm - 8:30pm under our much experienced coach consisting of drills and match practice. You can get the sporting equipment from your respective hostels and same can be procured from the institute once you get in the team.

The IIT Delhi TT Team takes part in a melange of tournaments throughout the year, thus giving the match temperament to the newbies required to convert matches into victories.The ones we participated in, in the last academic year were :-
The 51st Inter IIT meet, IIT Kanpur
BOSM, BITS Pilani
Sportech,IIT Delhi

It is also advised to play individual rankings to train yourself for the fierce inter college competitions.The coach holds regular friendly matches with competitive sports club at Delhi. The main aim of this is to give every player a chance to showcase his talent and strengthen the team through diverse challenges.
