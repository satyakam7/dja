IIT Delhi is proud to have 8 well maintained, floodlight courts: 4 hard courts and 4 clay courts.

The IIT Delhi Tennis ladder consists of 7 best players of the institute. Every fortnight matches are played amongst these players to determine the rankings. This makes the sport more competitive and always keeps players on their toes. The top four players of this ladder represent the college at tournaments. Come out and slowly climb your way up the top of the institute!

Court Timings:
Weekdays: 11am - 4pm | 6pm - 9pm
Weekends: 11am - 4pm | 6pm - 9pm

However, at any point of time 2 synthetic courts are reserved for the institute team. The Captain and Vice-captain of the institute are authorised to use the courts for the team’s practice at their disposal.
