Our IIT Delhi Badminton team has players as good as they get, and in spite of the infrastructural inefficiencies (hopefully the situation will change soon, with the new SAC being built :D), ours is the most consistent team in terms of performance. We have been consistently getting podium finishes at the prestigious Inter IIT’s (both the men's and women's team got a taste of gold in 2013) and our team is considered the favorite anywhere we participate (4 consecutive Sportech titles for the men's team and 7 gold medals in 11 tournaments we have taken part in the last 4 years, speak for themselves). Both our teams are a force to reckon with!!

The team practises​ every day (on the weekends as well) , especially in the Inter IIT Semester (yes, the odd semesters are the Inter IIT Semesters for us). The venue is the SAC Badminton Court, and the timings are from 5:30 PM to 9 PM. We have an excellent coach to help us take the right steps (no puns intended :D ). If you have feelings for the sport as well, and have a knack for hitting the right shots, this is the best place for you to spend your evenings. See you on the court!!
